Data Card

Designed using Automotive grade Microcontroller,has data flash for storing the on board design,battery powered real time clock for storing current pick information [pick resume on power on facility] . Circuit and PCB specially designed to handle large currents and regulate the supply to each leg for consistent operation, hence achieving error free weaving of designs . The data card consists of 7 legs(connectors), to each leg 16/12/9/8 relay cards can be connected, depending on hooks required . Opto isolated Sensor Inputs . Seven segment display to show the current PICK and connection status .

Relay Card

->Each Relay card controls Staubli 8 hook modules . ->Gold Plated contacts for long life and error free contacts . ->8 LEDs(low power) to indicate data, useful for trouble shooting . ->Optical isolation of power to magnets(15V) and Logic Circuits(5V) for error free control (this being our unique feature, helps in error free design transfer for weaving) and circuit protection against power surges . ->We use ODD & EVEN cards to eliminate FRC cable rework . ->Special circuit to reduce interference effects . ->Original imported SMDs are used, Machine Soldering is done . ->Testing using Automated Test Equipments(ATEs).

Embedded Controller

->This controller unit is built around ARM 32 bit processor clicking at 120MHz . ->User friendly software to carry out all operations such as Design file transfer, view design, delete file..etc . ->Online display of the current picks with design updates . ->On thread cut or on other occasions, user can Stop the unit(Unlock it) and then navigate through the picks and Start(lock it) the unit again . ->Copy design files from USB pen drive to Internal SD card memory as and when required . ->Remote Design file transfer through Ethernet port using FTP . ->Can control upto 2688 Hooks using 3data controller cards by using RS232 ports,using RS485 port the unit can control any number of Hooks by using multiple data cards .

Computer Based Controller

->Using SaiLoomControl Software, Desktop,Touch based Computer,Laptop, Industrial Tablet can be used as controller for opening,sending design files to Data cards . ->Start and Stop the Loom, PICK scrolling and Setting . Setting the Breaks . ->Facility to view proprietary Loom format file or colored bitmap file . ->Online Design Scrolling as the weaving progresses . ->Remote Monitoring and File Copying .

Loom Conversion

->Windows based Software developed using C#.Net Technology . ->Special Routines / Codes incorporated for Speedy Mixing and Handling of Large Designs . ->Apply any color to any design before Mixing . ->Multi Color Mixing (Mixing of Design with Zari and Meena..), Option to Choose mixing type like 1by1,1by2, 2by2, 4 by4 (Also we support any customizable types) . ->The Final Mixed Design can be up to 1 Lakh picks as a single repeat( that means Entire saree with complete pallu and border and butta arranged and blouse for the entire length of 5.5mts including multi pick details is stored as single repeat) .
