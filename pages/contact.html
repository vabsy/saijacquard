RELAY CARD :

Each Relay card controls Staubli 8 hook modules .

Gold Plated contacts for long life and error free contacts .

8 LEDs(low power) to indicate data, useful for trouble shooting .

Optical isolation of power to magnets(15V) and Logic Circuits(5V) for error free control (this being our unique feature, helps in error free design transfer for weaving) and circuit protection against power surges.

We use ODD & EVEN cards to eliminate FRC cable rework .

EMBEDDED CONTROLLER :

This controller unit is built around ARM 32 bit processor clicking at 120MHz .

User friendly software to carry out all operations such as Design file transfer, view design, delete file..etc .

Online display of the current picks with design updates .

On thread cut or on other occasions, user can Stop the unit(Unlock it) and then navigate through the picks and Start(lock it) the unit again.

Copy design files from USB pen drive to Internal SD card memory as and when required .

Remote Design file transfer through Ethernet port using FTP .
